A results-driven Senior Technical Architect and recognised Subject Matter Expert (SME) with over 20 years of progressive experience architecting, transforming, and securing enterprise-scale technology environments across the UK and globally.

Currently serving at NatWest Group Plc, I operate at the intersection of cloud strategy, infrastructure modernisation, and digital workplace transformation — designing solutions that balance innovation with regulatory rigour in one of the UK's most complex financial environments.

My career spans senior-level positions at Cognizant Technology Solutions and GFT Technologies, where I led transformational programmes including full-scale cloud migrations, VDI deployments, and DevSecOps platform builds for global enterprises. Prior to that, I built a deep foundation across Windows Server, Active Directory, and network infrastructure across diverse enterprise clients in India.

I specialise in bridging the gap between business strategy and technical delivery — translating complex requirements into resilient, scalable, and secure architectures across Azure, AWS, GCP, and hybrid on-premises estates. I am equally comfortable in a hands-on technical design role as I am advising at board and C-suite level on technology strategy.

Outside of architecture work, I am a passionate advocate for knowledge-sharing and continual learning — reflected through my technical blog on ApexDigits.com, mentoring junior architects, and active participation in the enterprise cloud community.
